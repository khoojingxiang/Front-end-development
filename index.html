Rise to greatness, rule the battlefield.

Each player can control a selectable character, called a Hero, with unique abilities and traits. There are six roles that define the main purpose of heroes: Tank, Marksman, Assassin, Fighter, Mage, and Support. These roles determine the responsibilities of players for their respective teams. ABOUT MPL SG

MPL Singapore is the only professional esports league in the country that aims to:

1. Enable professional esports athletes to compete at the highest level of national competition
2. Deliver the most meaningful, lasting, and unique sport entertainment experiences to fans.

It serves as a pathway for MLBB players to turn professional, where teams compete for a SGD 100,000 prize pool and stand a chance to qualify to regional and global MLBB Esports tournaments, such as the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Southeast Asia Cup and the M-Series World Championship.

MPL SG is back for its fifth season. Who will emerge triumphant and lay claim to the title of MPL SG Season 5 Champion? Stay tuned to find out!
