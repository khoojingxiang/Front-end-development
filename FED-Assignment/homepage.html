FPS Games

First-person shooter (FPS) is a sub-genre of shooter video games centered on gun and other weapon-based combat in a first-person perspective, with the player experiencing the action through the eyes of the protagonist and controlling the player character in a three-dimensional space.

A MOBA, or Massive Online Battle Arena, typically puts a team of five players against another. Generally played on PC, MOBAs have the ultimate goal of destroying the enemy base. Players must work together to acquire items and territory using unique characters, each with their own advantages and weaknesses.

SPORTS

Sports gaming refers to video games that simulate playing or managing a sports team or event. Players can control individual players or teams and compete against other players or computer-controlled teams in various sports such as football, basketball, baseball, soccer, golf, and more. These games often feature realistic graphics and gameplay, as well as realistic statistics and player ratings. The goal is to provide a virtual representation of the sport and offer players a fun and engaging experience.

STRATEGY

Strategy games are a genre of video games that challenge players to make decisions and manage resources in order to achieve a goal. Players are often put in control of a faction or civilization and must make choices regarding resource management, diplomacy, and military strategy in order to succeed. The gameplay often involves slow-paced, turn-based decision making, with an emphasis on careful planning and resource management.
